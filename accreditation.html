For Training Providers

IANA welcomes training providers who are committed to excellence in Neuro Acupuncture education. As the professional body dedicated to advancing Neuro Acupuncture worldwide, we provide accreditation services that recognise training programmes meeting our rigorous professional standards.

Why Partner with IANA?

IANA accreditation demonstrates your commitment to high-quality Neuro Acupuncture education and provides recognition from the international professional body for Neuro Acupuncture practitioners.

Professional Recognition

IANA accreditation demonstrates that your training programme meets internationally recognised professional standards in Neuro Acupuncture education.

Student Pathways

Students enrolled in IANA-accredited courses are eligible for IANA student membership and can transition to full professional membership upon completion.

Quality Assurance

Accreditation provides independent verification of educational quality, curriculum content, and clinical training standards.

Marketing Credibility

Use the IANA accreditation badge and logo in your marketing materials to demonstrate professional credibility to prospective students.

IANA Training Provider Membership

Before seeking course accreditation, training organisations must first become IANA training provider members. This membership establishes your organisation's commitment to professional standards in Neuro Acupuncture education and provides access to IANA's accreditation services.

Accreditation Process

Two-Step Process

Step 1: Training Provider Membership

Apply for IANA training provider membership. Your organisation will be reviewed to ensure it meets the requirements for delivering Neuro Acupuncture education.

Step 2: Course Accreditation Application

Once accepted as a training provider member, you may submit applications for individual course accreditation. Each course will be assessed against IANA's professional standards.

Fees & Timelines

Training Provider Membership

£240/year

  • Prerequisite for course accreditation
  • IANA training provider designation
  • Access to provider resources
  • Professional development updates
  • Support and guidance

Application review: 3-5 working days

Course Accreditation

£100/course/year

  • Individual course assessment
  • Curriculum review and verification
  • IANA accreditation certificate
  • Use of IANA accreditation badge
  • Course listing on IANA website

Accreditation review: 10-15 working days (when requirements met)

Application Requirements

Training Provider Membership Requirements

  • Registered training organisation with appropriate credentials
  • Professional indemnity insurance for training provision
  • Qualified teaching staff with documented Neuro Acupuncture expertise
  • Commitment to IANA standards and code of ethics
  • Appropriate facilities and equipment for Neuro Acupuncture training

Course Accreditation Requirements

Courses submitted for IANA accreditation must demonstrate:

  • Comprehensive curriculum covering recognised Neuro Acupuncture modalities
  • Adequate clinical practice hours and supervision
  • Evidence-based teaching approaches
  • Assessment methods that ensure clinical competence
  • Neurological assessment and safety protocols
  • Documented learning outcomes aligned with IANA standards
  • Integration of anatomy and physiology relevant to neurological acupuncture

[Image: Accreditation certificate / Quality assurance badge]

Application Process

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Review Requirements: Carefully review the requirements listed above
  2. Prepare Documentation: Gather all required documentation including curriculum materials, staff credentials, and facility information
  3. Apply for Training Provider Membership: Submit your training provider membership application with the £200 annual fee using the form below
  4. Await Review: Your application will be reviewed within 3-5 working days
  5. Submit Course Accreditation Applications: Once accepted as a training provider member, submit individual course accreditation applications (£150 per course)
  6. Accreditation Review: Course applications are reviewed within 10-15 working days when all requirements are met
  7. Receive Accreditation: Upon approval, receive your accreditation certificate and authorisation to use the IANA accreditation badge
