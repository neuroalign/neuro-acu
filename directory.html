About This Directory

All practitioners listed in the IANA directory meet the following criteria:

  • Current professional registration with recognised acupuncture regulatory body
  • Documented specialist training in recognised neuro acupuncture modalities
  • Active IANA membership in good standing
  • Maintenance of professional indemnity insurance
  • Commitment to continuing professional development
  • Adherence to IANA code of ethics and professional conduct

Disclaimer: IANA provides this directory as a professional resource. Patients are advised to verify practitioner credentials and discuss specific treatment requirements before commencing care. IANA does not provide clinical recommendations or guarantee treatment outcomes.
