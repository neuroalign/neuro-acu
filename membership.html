Membership Requirements

To join IANA as a professional member, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Educational Requirements: Hold a valid acupuncture license or certification from an accredited institution
  • Specialized Training: Have completed training in at least one recognized neuro-acupuncture modality (Zhu, Fang, or Tang scalp acupuncture, NeuroRelief, NeuroRehab, or NeuroBalance methods)
  • Clinical Experience: Minimum of 100 hours of clinical practice in neuro-acupuncture techniques
  • Professional Standing: Maintain current malpractice insurance and be in good standing with your licensing board
  • Ethical Standards: Agree to uphold IANA's code of ethics and professional conduct

Student Member Requirements:

  • Currently enrolled in an accredited acupuncture or Traditional Chinese Medicine program
  • Provide proof of enrollment (student ID or letter from institution)
  • Interest in pursuing neuro-acupuncture specialization

Application Process

Follow these steps to apply for IANA membership:

  1. Review the membership requirements and benefits above
  2. Choose your membership level (Student, Professional, or Lifetime)
  3. Complete the application form below
  4. Submit required documentation (license, certifications, proof of training)
  5. Pay the membership fee
  6. Await approval from the IANA membership committee (typically 5-7 business days)
  7. Receive your member certificate and access to member resources
