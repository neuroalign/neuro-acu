Find IANA-accredited neuro acupuncture training programmes delivered by recognised training providers who meet our rigorous educational standards.

Understanding Training Provider Status

IANA-Accredited Training Providers: Fully accredited by IANA with comprehensive quality assurance. All courses from these providers meet IANA professional standards and qualify graduates for IANA membership.

IANA-Recognised Training Providers: Specific courses from these providers are accepted by IANA towards meeting individual membership requirements. Additional training hours may be required to satisfy full membership criteria.

The following providers offer specific courses that IANA recognises towards meeting membership requirements. Courses totalling at least 100 hours from accredited or recognised providers are sufficient for IANA membership. Courses under 100 hours require additional training as specified below.
