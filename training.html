Find IANA-accredited neuro acupuncture training programmes delivered by recognised training providers who meet our rigorous educational standards.

About IANA-Accredited Training

All training programmes listed here have been assessed and accredited by IANA, ensuring they meet our professional standards for curriculum, practical training, assessment, and qualification outcomes.

IANA accreditation provides assurance that training equips students with the knowledge and skills necessary to practice neuro acupuncture safely and effectively.
