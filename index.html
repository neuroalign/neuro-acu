Professional Standards in Neuro-Acupuncture

The International Association for Neuro Acupuncture (IANA) represents practitioners of specialised neurological acupuncture modalities worldwide. Our organisation maintains rigorous professional standards whilst supporting clinical excellence through continuing professional development, research dissemination, and collaborative practice.

Professional Development

Access to continuing professional development programmes, advanced training workshops, and specialist certification pathways designed to enhance clinical practice and patient outcomes.

International Network

Membership of a global community of neuro-acupuncture specialists, facilitating knowledge exchange and collaborative research initiatives.

Research & Evidence Base

Access to current research, clinical studies, and evidence-based protocols in neuro-acupuncture practice and neurological rehabilitation.

Clinical Excellence

Support for practitioners committed to the highest standards of patient care, professional ethics, and clinical governance.

Recognised Neuro-Acupuncture Modalities

IANA recognises practitioners trained in established neuro-acupuncture approaches, including scalp acupuncture systems and contemporary neurological acupuncture protocols:

Scalp Acupuncture Systems

Zhu's Scalp Acupuncture

Comprehensive scalp microsystem developed by Dr Zhu Ming-Qing, utilising specific scalp zones corresponding to neurological functions.

Fang's Scalp Acupuncture

Advanced scalp acupuncture technique by Dr Fang Yun-Peng, focusing on precise scalp microsystem mapping.

Tang's Scalp Acupuncture

Specialised scalp acupuncture approach by Dr Tang Song-Yan, emphasising specific stimulation techniques.

YNSA

Yamamoto New Scalp Acupuncture, a refined microsystem approach developed by Dr Toshikatsu Yamamoto.

NeuroAlign Methods

Contemporary evidence-based protocols for neurological conditions and rehabilitation:

NeuroRelief Method

Targeted protocols for neurological pain management and symptom relief.

NeuroRehab Method

Structured rehabilitation protocols for neurological recovery and functional restoration.

NeuroBalance Method

Integrative approaches for neurological homeostasis and functional optimisation.
