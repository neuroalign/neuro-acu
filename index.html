What is Neuro Acupuncture?

Neuro acupuncture is a specialised approach focusing on brain and nervous system function to treat neurological conditions, chronic pain, and functional limitations. Using precise techniques—particularly scalp acupuncture—it influences central nervous system activity to promote recovery.

IANA establishes internationally recognised professional standards, accredits training programmes, and advances evidence-based practice to ensure patients receive safe, effective care from qualified practitioners.

Conditions Treated with Neuro Acupuncture

Neuro acupuncture treats a wide range of neurological conditions, helping to reduce symptoms, improve recovery, and enhance quality of life:

Stroke Recovery

Supports motor recovery, coordination, and functional restoration following stroke.

Movement Disorders

Helps reduce tremor and improve motor function in Parkinson's disease and related conditions.

Chronic Pain

Addresses musculoskeletal and neuropathic pain by modulating pain processing in the brain.

Migraine & Headaches

Reduces frequency and intensity by rebalancing nervous system function.

Multiple Sclerosis

Manages fatigue, spasticity, sensory disturbances, and mobility limitations.

Neurological Rehabilitation

Supports recovery from traumatic brain injury, peripheral neuropathy, balance disorders, and cognitive difficulties.

Recognised Neuro Acupuncture Modalities

IANA recognises practitioners trained in established Neuro Acupuncture approaches, including scalp acupuncture systems and contemporary neurological acupuncture protocols:

NeuroAlign Methods

Contemporary evidence-based protocols specifically developed to address neurological conditions and chronic pain through targeted nervous system modulation:

NeuroRelief Method

Brain-centred scalp acupuncture approach for chronic pain management, targeting pain-processing regions to provide lasting relief.

NeuroRehab Method

Structured rehabilitation protocol using scalp acupuncture and movement techniques to support functional recovery following neurological injury or disease.

NeuroBalance Method

Auricular (ear) acupuncture approach for mental health conditions, emotional dysregulation, and addiction.

Scalp Acupuncture Systems

Zhu's Scalp Acupuncture

Comprehensive scalp microsystem developed by Dr Zhu Ming-Qing, utilising specific scalp zones corresponding to neurological functions.

Fang's Scalp Acupuncture

Advanced scalp acupuncture technique by Dr Fang Yun-Peng, focusing on precise scalp microsystem mapping.

Tang's Scalp Acupuncture

Specialised scalp acupuncture approach by Dr Tang Song-Yan, emphasising specific stimulation techniques.

YNSA

Yamamoto New Scalp Acupuncture, a refined microsystem approach developed by Dr Toshikatsu Yamamoto.
