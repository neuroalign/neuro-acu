Welcome to IANA

The International Association for Neuro Acupuncture (IANA) is a professional organization dedicated to supporting practitioners of neuro-acupuncture modalities worldwide. We unite practitioners who specialize in advanced neurological acupuncture techniques to promote excellence in patient care, professional development, and clinical research.

Neuro-Acupuncture Modalities

IANA supports practitioners of various neuro-acupuncture techniques, including:

Scalp Acupuncture

Including Zhu's Scalp Acupuncture, Fang's Scalp Acupuncture, and Tang's Scalp Acupuncture techniques for neurological conditions

NeuroRelief Method

Advanced techniques for managing neurological pain and discomfort

NeuroRehab Method

Specialized protocols for neurological rehabilitation and recovery

NeuroBalance Method

Integrative approaches for neurological balance and functional optimization
