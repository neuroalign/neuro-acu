First Aid Training Providers

Find recognised first aid training providers approved by IANA for delivering first aid qualifications required for neuro acupuncture practice.

First Aid Requirements for IANA Membership

All IANA Full Members are required to maintain current first aid certification as part of their professional competence requirements. Valid first aid qualifications must include:

  • Certification from a recognised first aid training provider
  • Current, in-date qualification (typically requiring renewal every 3 years)
  • Coverage of emergency response procedures relevant to clinical practice
  • CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation) certification

Other First Aid Qualifications

Case-by-Case Assessment: First aid certificates from other recognised providers will be assessed on a case-by-case basis during the membership application process.

International Applicants: In principle, if a first aid certificate is valid and recognised in the applicant's country of practice, it will be accepted by IANA. Members are required to maintain current, in-date first aid certification appropriate to their jurisdiction.

For questions regarding specific first aid qualifications, please contact the IANA membership team.

Recommended First Aid Courses

IANA members practicing in the UK should typically hold one of the following first aid qualifications:

  • First Aid at Work (FAW) - 3-day comprehensive course providing extensive first aid knowledge for workplace first aiders
  • Emergency First Aid at Work (EFAW) - 1-day course covering essential first aid skills and emergency response
  • Level 3 Award in First Aid at Work - Ofqual-regulated qualification meeting Health and Safety Executive requirements

All qualifications must include CPR training and be current (not expired). Certificates typically require renewal every 3 years.
