First Aid Training Providers

Find recognised first aid training providers approved by IANA for delivering first aid qualifications required for neuro acupuncture practice.

First Aid Requirements for IANA Membership

All IANA Full Members are required to maintain current first aid certification as part of their professional competence requirements. Valid first aid qualifications must include:

  • Certification from a recognised first aid training provider
  • Current, in-date qualification (typically requiring renewal every 3 years)
  • Coverage of emergency response procedures relevant to clinical practice
  • CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation) certification

Recognised First Aid Training Providers

Our directory of recognised first aid training providers is currently being developed. This will include providers who offer appropriate first aid qualifications for healthcare practitioners.

In the meantime, we recognise first aid qualifications from the following types of providers:

  • St John Ambulance
  • British Red Cross
  • St Andrew's First Aid (Scotland)
  • Regulated first aid training providers offering Ofqual-accredited qualifications
  • Equivalent recognised providers in other jurisdictions

Recommended First Aid Courses

IANA members should hold one of the following first aid qualifications:

  • First Aid at Work (FAW) - 3-day comprehensive course providing extensive first aid knowledge
  • Emergency First Aid at Work (EFAW) - 1-day course covering essential first aid skills
  • Level 3 Award in First Aid at Work - Ofqual-regulated qualification

All qualifications must include CPR and be current (not expired).

Questions About First Aid Requirements?

If you have questions about which first aid qualifications are acceptable for IANA membership, or need guidance on selecting appropriate first aid training, please contact us.
