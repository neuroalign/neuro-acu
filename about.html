What is the IANA?

The International Association for Neuro Acupuncture (IANA) is a professional organization dedicated to advancing the practice and understanding of neuro-acupuncture worldwide. We serve as a central hub for practitioners who specialize in neurological acupuncture modalities, fostering excellence in clinical practice, education, and research.

Our Mission

IANA's mission is to promote the highest standards of neuro-acupuncture practice through:

  • Supporting professional development and continuing education for practitioners
  • Facilitating collaboration and knowledge sharing among neuro-acupuncture specialists
  • Advancing clinical research and evidence-based practice
  • Raising awareness of neuro-acupuncture among healthcare professionals and the public
  • Maintaining ethical and professional standards in the field

What is Neuro-Acupuncture?

Neuro-acupuncture is a specialized branch of acupuncture that focuses on treating neurological conditions and optimizing neurological function. It combines traditional acupuncture principles with modern understanding of neurology and neurophysiology.

Neuro-acupuncture techniques are particularly effective for:

  • Stroke recovery and rehabilitation
  • Traumatic brain injury
  • Parkinson's disease and movement disorders
  • Multiple sclerosis
  • Peripheral neuropathy
  • Chronic neurological pain
  • Balance and coordination disorders
  • Cognitive function enhancement

Recognized Modalities

IANA recognizes and supports practitioners of various neuro-acupuncture modalities, including:

Zhu's Scalp Acupuncture

A comprehensive scalp acupuncture system developed by Dr. Zhu Ming-Qing, utilizing specific scalp zones corresponding to neurological functions and conditions.

Fang's Scalp Acupuncture

An advanced scalp acupuncture technique developed by Dr. Fang Yun-Peng, focusing on precise scalp microsystem mapping for neurological treatment.

Tang's Scalp Acupuncture

A specialized approach to scalp acupuncture by Dr. Tang Song-Yan, emphasizing specific stimulation techniques for optimal neurological outcomes.

NeuroRelief Method

A modern protocol specifically designed for managing neurological pain and discomfort using targeted acupuncture techniques.

NeuroRehab Method

An evidence-based rehabilitation protocol utilizing neuro-acupuncture for recovery from neurological injuries and conditions.

NeuroBalance Method

An integrative approach focusing on restoring neurological balance and optimizing brain-body communication through acupuncture.
